About the Archive

Curated Statement

Urban Identity is a curated photography archive focused on identity, belonging, and everyday life in New York City. Through multiple thematic galleries, the project examines how individuals experience themselves within shared urban spaces.

Curatorial Approach

The archive is organized into five thematic collections, each offering a different lens on urban life. Together, the galleries highlight moments of presence, transition, and self-expression within the city.

Purpose and Audience

This project is intended as a digital gallery experience that encourages reflection and slow viewing, treating photography as both documentation and personal expression.
