Galleries

This archive is organized into five thematic photography collections exploring identity and urban life in New York City.

Identity & Belonging

Portraits and scenes that explore personal, cultural, and social identity as shaped by life in New York City.

Urban Moments

Everyday moments and candid scenes that capture the rhythm, diversity, and lived experience of the city.

The Quiet In-Between

Photographs of stillness, transition, and overlooked spaces within the city's constant movement.

Body, Form, Expression

A collection focused on light, shadow, and atmosphere as defining elements of the urban environment.

Light as Subject
